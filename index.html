Welcome to GQ

We, the Gentlemen of Quality are a brotherhood dedicated to the principles of leadership, service and scholarship. We seek the academic, social, and cultural enhancements of our communities irrespective of race, color, creed, sexuality, and religion. We recognize, respect, and affirm the diverse backgrounds that bring us together to positively impact the NYU community. We aspire to set standards that will be built upon by future leaders. Through these principles, we strive to keep inspiring and nurturing a gentlemen's spirit.

OUR FOUNDATION

Our 3 Pillars

Leadership

Leadership is at the core of GQ's mission. We provide opportunities for our members to develop their leadership skills through workshops, discussions, and showing leadership through example. We aspire to set standards that will be built upon by future leaders. We encourage our members to take initiative, collaborate with others, and make a positive impact on the NYU community and beyond.

Service

GQ is committed to giving back to the community through various service initiatives. We organize volunteer opportunities, community outreach programs, and partnerships with high schools and local organizations to make a positive impact. Our members actively participate in service projects that address social issues, promote education, and support underprivileged communities.

Scholarship

At GQ we have high academic standards and provide resources to help our members excel academically. We offer study groups and tutoring sessions to help you succeed. We also host an academic check-in meeting every semester to provide support and guidance for your academic journey.
