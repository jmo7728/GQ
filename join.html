HOW TO JOIN

Become a Member

Complete the following steps to officially become part of our organization.

  1. Attend Six (6) general meetings including two (2) political action meetings where we meet every Friday from 6:00PM to 8:00PM @ 25 W 4th St Rm 602
  2. Attend Two (2) community service engagements, which may be fulfilled by participating in AAP, GQ, WEST, or OP community service initiatives
  3. Complete Title IX and Consent Zone trainings
  4. Take the GQ Member Quiz
  5. Recite the GQ mission statement and plan a new member event
